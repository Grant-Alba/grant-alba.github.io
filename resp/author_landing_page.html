About Me

Welcome to my world of storytelling! I'm passionate about crafting tales that transcend convential genres boundaries.

With over thirty years as a global consultant, I've written far too many words simply for the purpose of commerce. I've taken my global experience and corporate writing experience, to craft carefully plotted tales of intrigue, filled with smart unconventional characters. I specialize in weaving together elements of crime thriller, science fiction, romance, and human connection. When I'm not writing, you'll find me baking bread, eating that bread, sketching, riding my road bike, playing backgammon, or getting lost in a book (honestly? words in a line are all I need)
